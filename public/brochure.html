Welcome to

MLRInstitute of Technology

Established in 2005, MLR Institute of Technology (MLRIT) has emerged as one of Telangana's leading institutions committed to academic excellence, innovation, and holistic development. With a vision to nurture competent engineers and future-ready leaders, the institute has consistently upheld high standards in education, research, and industry engagement.

At MLRIT, learning goes beyond traditional classroom teaching. The institution adopts a dynamic approach that integrates strong theoretical foundations with hands-on practical exposure, encouraging students to think critically, innovate, and solve real-world problems. This balanced pedagogy has enabled graduates to excel in information technology, core engineering sectors, research, entrepreneurship, and management.

The institute is supported by a team of highly qualified and experienced faculty, modern infrastructure, well-equipped laboratories, and a vibrant academic environment. Strong collaborations with industry and research organizations further enhance students' exposure to emerging technologies and professional practices.

Driven by a commitment to quality and continuous improvement, MLRIT focuses on developing not just skilled professionals, but responsible individuals with strong ethical values and a global outlook.

At MLRIT, education is not merely about earning a degree — it is about shaping minds, inspiring innovation, and building the leaders of tomorrow.

How to Reach Us

MLR Institute of Technology (MLRIT)
Nurturing Talent.
Enabling Excellence.

MLRIT enjoys a prime geographical location with excellent connectivity by air, rail and road.

The campus is located in Pundigal, Quthbullapur Mandal, Medchal–Malkajgiri District, Hyderabad, adjacent to Outer Ring Road (ORR) Exit No. 5 (Medchal). This makes MLRIT easily accessible from all major parts of the city.

The institute is 20 kms away from Secunderabad Railway Station, 11 km from JNTUH (Kukatpally) and about 40 minutes' drive from Rajiv Gandhi International Airport, Shamshabad.
